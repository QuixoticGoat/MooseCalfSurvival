title:
	Estimating factors influencing moose calf survival at Alaska Peninsula-Becharof Refuges	

subject: 
        moose, calf, survival, known-fate

description:
	We sought to estimate moose calf survival using data from radio-collared moose. We employed
	a known-fate model using the Rmarkdown package in R. We investigated the role of environmental
	covariates on calf survival.
